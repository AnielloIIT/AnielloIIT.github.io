About Me

BIO

Hello! My name is Aniello Scartozzi and this is my website. I was born in Rochester Hills Michigan and have a unique passion for technology. I built this website to showcase my passion for technology while also providing a medium for anyone to see my work or find my contacts. I am interested in all fields of information technology but I am specificity interested in the internet of things, full stack development, hardware, white hat testing, front and back end development. Below you can find more specifics about myself.

Web Development

HTML, CSS, Javascript

I have been creating websites for about 6 years now. I keep my websites optimized and modern using up to date web markup. My websites are build mobile first so they will look good across a wide array of devices. This ensures that my sites can be seen by everyone.

Backend Development

Shell Scripting, Linux, Databases

My passion also lies with back end development. This involves installing operating systems on machines to build a network backends. This includes maintaining and securing networks. I can use Linux to do anything from serving web pages to setting up email servers.

White Hat Tester

Ethical Hacking

I also indulge in white hat testing which is a fancy term for an ethical hacker. I stay up on this in order to maintain security for my websites, programs and networks. The only way to have a good defense is by knowing the game of the offense.
